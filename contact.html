Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply for admission through our online portal or by visiting our admissions office in person. The application process requires submitting your academic transcripts, identification documents, and completing the application form.

What are the tuition fees?

Tuition fees vary by program and level of study. Please contact our finance office for detailed information about tuition fees, payment plans, and available scholarships.

Do you offer online courses?

Yes, we offer a variety of online and hybrid courses. Please check our course catalog or contact the academic affairs office for more information about our online learning options.

How can I schedule a campus tour?

You can schedule a campus tour by contacting our admissions office. We offer guided tours Monday through Friday and on select Saturdays.
