Business Administration Program

Program Overview

Our Business Administration program provides students with a comprehensive understanding of business principles and practices. The curriculum covers areas such as management, marketing, finance, accounting, and entrepreneurship.

Career Opportunities

  • Business Manager
  • Marketing Specialist
  • Financial Analyst
  • Entrepreneur
  • Human Resources Manager

Program Requirements

To graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, students must complete 120 credit hours, including general education requirements, core business courses, and elective courses in their area of interest.
